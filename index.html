Our Roots

Fueled by "flyover country" pride and inspired by middle America's most classic dessert, NoCoast Pie Co. is a Minneapolis bakery devoted to creating high quality pies with fresh ingredients and original flavors.

All About The Pie

We believe a good pie starts with a great crust, and we are firmly in the all-butter crust camp. Our pies rotate weekly, but a couple of our specialties are Maple Oatmeal Breakfast Pie, Chocolate Brownie Chess, Rhubarb Chess, and Strawberry Almond Crumble. Follow us on social media to see what we're baking this week!
